About Villa24

In the heart of Vlora

Where history, the sea and urban life meet, you'll find Villa24 — a space created with care and dedication. Set on Rruga “7 Nëntori” in the centre of Vlora, in a quiet and characterful area, it sits about 600 m from Muradie Square, the historic heart of the city, and around 1.5 km from the sea — so you can explore Vlora easily on foot.

Villa24 is an elegant, calm structure with 4 carefully furnished rooms, each with a private bathroom — combining simplicity, cleanliness and warm hospitality in an intimate, modern setting.

Vlora is one of the most important cities in Albanian history. It was here, on 28 November 1912, that the Independence of Albania was proclaimed. Today the city blends history and culture with the sea, nature, gastronomy and modern life.
